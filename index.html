The United States of America

The United States of America is a North American nation that is the world’s most dominant economic and military power. It's famous for its attractions, such as the Grand Canyon, tech innovation, sports, and it has a large imprint on the global culture thanks to famous movies, television shows, and music. A shining bastion of hope and Democracy in the world. A place where the streets are paved with gold and all men are created equal. Where blacks, Muslims, gays, Jews and all other races, religious creeds, and sexual orientations are accepted. A land where every man, woman, and child has a roof over their head and food on the table. A place where anyone can be anything they imagine simply from sheer determination and ingenuity. Where every man can succeed and prosper. Not only a nation, but a state of mind that every human being should aspire to.
